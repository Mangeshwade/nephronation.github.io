At Home Dialysis

Hemodialysis can be done at home too! The following are the advantages of doing Hemodialysis at home.


1. Dialyse at your own convenience.
2. No need to adhere to center schedules and timings.
3. Be completely independent.

You will need the following

1. Hemodialysis machine.
